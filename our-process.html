Our process

  • Step 1: Build a target demo

    If you care about gamers, then you have to reach them where they are. Lucky for you, we are connected to over 100M gamers globally who interact with our bots on Discord.

    We need to identify which types of gamers you care about:

    • casual vs core
    • PC vs console vs mobile
    • interests (anime, tech, cars - you name it)

  • Step 2: Attract an audience

    Once we know the target profile that would enjoy interacting with your brand, we begin alerting users that your new community is up and running. For most gamers, this is not enough of a draw...

    Gamers care about their favorite game (obvs right?). So if a gamer matches your target profile, then we’ll send out a message like so.

  • Step 3: Retain loyal fans

    Whether we are building a server from scratch or operating an existing server, we assign a dedicated live ops manager to ensure tournaments run smoothly, mods stay in line and ensure members are happy.

    Just like announcing a server’s availability is not enough to draw most people in, interactions with fans beyond a daily esports tournaments are a must.

    Our live ops team will run meme and trivia contests, stream music (everyone loves being DJ for a day) and much more. Exports is the hook for gamers to join the community. High touch interactions keep them there.

  • Step 4: Enjoy!

    New targeting options abound with your community of gamers. You can market new products, solicit feedback from the core audience, boost social view counts etc.

    The opportunities for communicating with this dedicated audience and fanbase are only limited by your marketing teams vision.

    The best part is: there are no costs associated with marketing to your users. These are your users - they know and trust your brand through this community...so go get ‘em tiger!
