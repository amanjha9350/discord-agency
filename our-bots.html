Our bots

We own and operate some of the most beloved and used bots on Discord. Whats a bot? Think of it like a plug-in that can be added by any discord community

  • Moderation

    Moderation bots handles hundreds of routine tasks in the background for admins and mods like:

    • role setting (who has access to which parts of the server)
    • stat collection
    • naughty word, NSFW image filtering
    • ban hammers
    • and more

  • Gaming

    Gaming bots are the lifeblood of the server. Attracting an audience to the server by offering esports tournaments is the most important thing when building+operating a server for your brand. Gamers love to brag and connecting their accounts to show off their skillz is a fun add-on too. A simple LFG tool makes all the difference in keeping gamers on your server for the longterm too.

  • Music

    Music bots stream music from any connected service a user chooses. Certain roles can even be the DJ for the whole server to listen to their sweet tunes.

  • Anime/Memes

    Anime/Meme bots keep the good times rolling with users sending upwards of 1M memes every single day to each other. These bots are super effective at entertaining users in between tournaments.
